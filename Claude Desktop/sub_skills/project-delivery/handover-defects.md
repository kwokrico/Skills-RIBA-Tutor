# Project Delivery: Handover + Defects (End-of-Contract Procedures)

## Purpose
Use for Practical Completion, Sectional Completion, Partial Possession, and the Rectification Period. These are frequent “Day 2” topics.

## A. Practical Completion (PC) checklist (prompt list)
- Works complete **except minor snagging**.\n- Safety-critical items complete.\n- Required certificates/approvals in place (as applicable).\n- Handover info ready (O&M/manuals as required).\n- Snagging list issued with clear responsibility and dates.\n\n## B. Sectional Completion
- Identify the section definition.\n- Confirm sectional PC criteria.\n- Confirm interface risks and handover sequencing.\n\n## C. Partial Possession
- Confirm contract mechanism allows it.\n- Identify consequences: insurance, risk transfer, access, damages.\n- Document clear boundaries and responsibilities.\n\n## D. Rectification Period / Defects
- Define defect vs snag vs latent defect.\n- Confirm reporting route and timescales.\n- Ensure defects lists are evidenced and tracked.\n\n## E. Common pitfalls (exam traps)
- Declaring PC too early (client pressure).\n- Incomplete statutory sign-off/commissioning.\n- Poor handover records.\n- Confusion on who carries insurance and risk after partial possession.\n\n## F. Output template
**Issue**: [PC/sectional/partial possession/defects]\n\n**Contractual mechanism**: [what needs to happen, who issues what]\n\n**Immediate actions**:\n- [bullet]\n- [bullet]\n\n**Evidence to record**:\n- [bullet]\n+
