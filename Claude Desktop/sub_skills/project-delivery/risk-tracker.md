# Project Delivery: Risk Tracker (Common UK Architectural Risks)

## Purpose
Use to quickly identify hazards and paste in exam-ready implications + mitigations.

## How to use
1) Match scenario cues to the risks below.\n2) Paste the “Implications” and tailor to facts.\n3) Add immediate actions + evidence to request.\n\n## Risk database (templates)

### Asbestos
- **Cues**: pre-2000 building, refurbishment, unknown surveys.\n- **Implications**: programme and cost risk; H&S duties; survey requirements.\n- **Immediate actions**: commission appropriate survey; stop intrusive works until confirmed; update risk register.\n- **Evidence**: asbestos register/survey; contractor RAMS.\n\n### RAAC
- **Cues**: public buildings/schools/hospitals; flat roof planks; structural concern.\n- **Implications**: structural safety risk; potential closure; urgent surveys.\n- **Immediate actions**: structural engineer; restrict access; temporary propping if advised.\n- **Evidence**: structural report; monitoring regime.\n\n### Party Wall etc.
- **Cues**: boundary works, excavation near neighbour, shared walls.\n- **Implications**: delays; neighbour dispute; additional professional fees.\n- **Immediate actions**: advise client; appoint surveyor; programme allowance.\n- **Evidence**: notices/awards; neighbour correspondence.\n\n### Heritage / Listing
- **Cues**: Grade II, conservation area, converted mill.\n- **Implications**: consent risks; specialist methods; redesign loops.\n- **Immediate actions**: conservation advice; pre-app; heritage statement.\n- **Evidence**: listing entry; conservation officer feedback.\n\n### Fire strategy interface
- **Cues**: change of use, higher occupancy, compartmentation queries.\n- **Implications**: compliance risk; late redesign; coordination burden.\n- **Immediate actions**: fire engineer early; design risk reviews.\n- **Evidence**: fire strategy; coordination notes.\n\n### Planning constraints
- **Cues**: tight site, conditions, neighbour objections.\n- **Implications**: programme uncertainty; scope changes.\n- **Immediate actions**: planning strategy; stakeholder engagement.\n- **Evidence**: decision notice; conditions tracker.\n+
