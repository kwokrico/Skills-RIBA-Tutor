# Legal & Safety: Building Safety Act 2022 + CDM 2015 (RAG Lists)

## Purpose
Use as a compliance checklist when answering risk assessments, dutyholder roles, HRB issues, and construction safety governance.

## A. BSA 2022: Gateway thinking (exam-safe)
Use these as **prompts/checkpoints** (do not over-quote if the paper gives limited facts).

### Gateway 1 (Planning stage – HRB lens)
- Ask: is the building a **Higher-Risk Building (HRB)**?\n- Evidence prompts: height/storeys/residential use; fire statement requirements (where applicable).\n\n### Gateway 2 (Before construction begins – “permission to start” mindset)
- Confirm dutyholders and competence.\n- Confirm design intent, information management, and governance suitable for HRB risk.\n\n### Gateway 3 (Completion / occupation – “permission to occupy” mindset)
- Confirm completion information, change control, and safe handover information.\n- Ensure the handover content is coherent and defensible.\n\n## B. CDM 2015: Dutyholder prompt list
- **Client** duties: appointments, adequate time/resources, ensure arrangements.\n- **Principal Designer**: plan/manage/monitor pre-construction; coordinate designers; eliminate/reduce risks.\n- **Principal Contractor**: construction phase plan; site controls; coordination.\n- **Designers**: eliminate/reduce/communicate residual risks.\n\n## C. CDM RAG list (use as a quick grading tool)
### Red (stop / urgent)
- Unclear dutyholder appointments on notifiable/complex project.\n- Known high-risk hazards ignored (asbestos/structural instability/fire strategy conflicts).\n- Unsafe instructions or pressure to bypass safety duties.\n\n### Amber (must control and document)
- Incomplete surveys / unknowns.\n- Programme pressure compressing safe planning.\n- Unclear information management / responsibilities.\n\n### Green (good practice)
- Clear appointments, competence evidence.\n- Risk workshops and design risk register.\n- Clear residual risk communication and H&S file approach.\n\n## D. Output template for “Risk Assessment” answers
**Applicability**: BSA/HRB? CDM dutyholders?\n\n**Key risks (RAG)**: [bullets]\n\n**Immediate actions**: [bullets]\n\n**Governance / evidence to keep**: [bullets]\n+
