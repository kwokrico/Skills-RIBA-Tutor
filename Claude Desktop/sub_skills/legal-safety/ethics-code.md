# Legal & Safety: Ethics Code Checker (ARB + RIBA)

## Purpose
Use to detect ethical breaches, conflicts of interest, competence issues, misleading statements, confidentiality breaches, or undue pressure from directors/clients/contractors.

## A. Quick checker (pattern triggers)
Flag high risk if you see:\n- “Just sign it”, “don’t put it in writing”, “backdate”, “hide defects”, “alter records”.\n- Gifts/hospitality that could compromise judgement.\n- Conflicts: side work, undisclosed relationships, acting for two parties.\n- Competence gaps: asked to do work beyond competence without support.\n- Confidential information shared inappropriately.\n
## B. ARB Standards (12) — summary headings
Use as a checklist; cite the relevant standard number in your advice.
1. Honesty and integrity\n2. Competence\n3. Professional knowledge and skill\n4. Managing your business/finances\n5. Insurance\n6. Managing your practice\n7. Relations with clients\n8. Handling complaints\n9. Maintaining confidentiality\n10. Conflict of interest\n11. Conduct with colleagues and others\n12. Maintaining trust in the profession\n
## C. RIBA Principles (3) — quick use
- **Integrity**\n- **Competence**\n- **Relationships**\n
## D. Output template
**Ethics risk rating**: Red / Amber / Green\n\n**Issue**: [one sentence]\n\n**Why it’s a breach risk**: [ARB standard(s) + RIBA principle]\n\n**Immediate actions**:\n- [bullet]\n- [bullet]\n\n**How to document defensively**:\n- [bullet]\n+
