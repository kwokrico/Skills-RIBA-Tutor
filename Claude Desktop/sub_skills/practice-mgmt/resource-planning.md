# Practice Management: Resource Planning (Capacity, Programming, Stage Hours)

## Purpose
Use for “Day 1” programming/capacity questions: whether NDG can take on work, and how long stages should take.

## A. Benchmark approach (keep defensible)
When asked “how long should this take?”, respond with **ranges** and state dependencies:\n- building type/complexity\n- client decision speed\n- statutory constraints (planning/heritage)\n- procurement route\n- extent of surveys/unknowns\n\n## B. Stage-hours benchmark table (template)

## C. Capacity check logic (quick)
- Convert demand to hours by stage.\n- Convert staff availability to **recoverable hours**.\n- Identify bottlenecks (e.g., Stage 4 tech lead shortage).\n- Propose mitigation: phased delivery, temporary support, sub-consultants, scope reduction, programme extension.\n\n### FTE conversion (simple)
\n\t\( \\text{FTE} \\approx \\dfrac{\\text{Total hours required over period}}{\\text{Available hours per person over period}} \\)\n\n## D. Programme output template
