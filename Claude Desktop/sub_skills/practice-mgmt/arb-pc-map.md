# Practice Management: ARB Professional Criteria Map (PC1–PC5)

## Purpose
Use at the **end of every answer** to signpost which ARB Professional Criteria are being demonstrated, and to add one “examiner signal” keyword/authority.

## Output format (mandatory)
Use this block at the end of the response:

**Criteria Mapping (ARB PC)**
- **PCx.y**: short evidence statement (what in *this* answer demonstrates it)
- **Examiner signal**: one keyword / legislation / case / guidance to name-drop appropriately

## Master list (candidate-facing)
Keep this high-level in the answer; be specific in the mapping.

### PC1 — Professionalism
- Ethics, integrity, competence, client care, conflicts, CPD, professional judgement.

### PC2 — Clients, users and delivery of services
- Briefing, appointments, scope, fee/value, procurement advice, communication, managing expectations.

### PC3 — Legal framework and processes
- Planning, building regs, CDM, contracts, statutory duties, dispute routes, duties/roles.

### PC4 — Practice and management
- Office resources, staffing, QA, risk management, financial management, PII awareness, business procedures.

### PC5 — Building procurement
- Procurement routes, tendering, contract administration, payment/time, handover/defects, warranties/third party rights.

## Mapping rubric (how to choose)
Use this decision logic:
- If you flagged **ethics/conflict/pressure** → include **PC1**.\n- If you advised on **appointment/scope/fees/client comms** → include **PC2**.\n- If you cited **statute/regs/planning/CDM/BSA** → include **PC3**.\n- If you did **practice audit, resourcing, capacity, or fee viability** → include **PC4**.\n- If you advised on **procurement/contract admin/payment/PC/defects/warranties** → include **PC5**.\n
## Examiner-signal library (pick one per answer)
Choose one relevant signal; do not spam.
- **Contracts/admin**: “Pay Less Notice”, “time-bar notice”, “contemporaneous records”.\n- **Planning/heritage**: “Listed Building Consent”, “heritage significance”, “NPPF public benefits”.\n- **CDM**: “client duties”, “pre-construction information”, “risk elimination hierarchy”.\n- **BSA/HRB**: “Gateways”, “dutyholders/competence”, “golden thread”.\n- **PII**: “claims-made basis”, “notification of circumstances”, “run-off cover”.\n- **Procurement**: “risk allocation”, “design responsibility”, “cost certainty vs control”.\n- **Handover**: “Practical Completion”, “Rectification Period”, “latent defects”.\n- **Sustainability**: “Part L compliance”, “SAP”, “RIBA 2030 Climate Challenge”.
